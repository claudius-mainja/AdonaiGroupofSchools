Who We Are

Adonai Group of Schools is a multi-level education institution committed to providing quality, accessible, and values-driven education. The group operates Adonai Primary School, Adonai High School, and Adonai Training Institute, offering a complete educational pathway from early learning to skills-based career training.

Our Objectives

To provide facilities for life development skills, values, and care for state children and foster parents for the benefit of all school youths and other vulnerable groups.

To absorb state children released from orphanages and children’s homes and empower them with life skills that can provide sustenance and income.
To mobilise resources for deployment to support youth-led enterprise development initiatives.
To employ resource persons from within the community who will provide technical skills training, entrepreneurship, and psycho-social support.
To network and cooperate with public, private sector, and civil society partners.
To produce finished products that will be sold to generate income for the centre in order to ensure project sustainability and continuity.
To undertake ancillary income-generating activities such as offering conferencing facilities to external parties.

Our Mission

Our Purpose

To alleviate the plight of state children in their vulnerable environments by making the requisite psychosocial assistance available for proper development and empowerment, and adult supervision.

We aim to train and equip future pastors, teachers, and leaders with the right skills, ethical values, and morals in order to empower themselves and others while spreading Christian values. This will be achieved by promoting Christian Values.
